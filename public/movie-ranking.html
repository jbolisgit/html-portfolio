The Best Movies According to James

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

This movie is a classic that has breathtaking scenes and a storyline that keeps your hooked the moment you start.

Cloud Atlas

This film has multiple storylines put in one that are in different time slots throughout history. They share an overarching theme where characters are linked throughout time.

Tomb Raider: 2004

This film is a classic action film with Angelina Jolie fitting the role perfectly.
